# Privacy Policy for Institute Transport Tracker

**Last Updated: November 27, 2025**

## Introduction

Institute Transport Tracker ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

---

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
We collect the following personal information when you register and use our app:

- **Email Address**: Used for account authentication and communication
- **Name**: Used to identify you within the app
- **Phone Number**: Used for WhatsApp notifications (optional)
- **Role**: Driver or Passenger designation
- **User ID**: Automatically generated unique identifier

### Location Information
We collect precise location data when you use our app:

- **Real-time GPS Location**: Collected when drivers start a trip
- **Location History**: Stored temporarily during active trips
- **Geofence Data**: Used to detect bus arrivals at stops

**When Location is Collected:**
- **Drivers**: Continuously during active trips (foreground service)
- **Passengers**: Only when viewing the map
- **Background**: Only for drivers during active trips with explicit permission

### Usage Information
We automatically collect certain information:

- **Device Information**: Device model, operating system version
- **App Usage**: Features used, trip data, timestamps
- **Crash Reports**: Anonymous crash logs via Firebase Crashlytics
- **Analytics**: App performance and usage statistics

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

### Core Functionality
- **Authentication**: Verify your identity and manage your account
- **Location Tracking**: Show real-time bus location to passengers
- **Notifications**: Send WhatsApp messages when bus arrives at stops
- **Route Management**: Allow drivers to create and manage routes
- **Geofencing**: Automatically detect bus arrivals

### Service Improvement
- **Analytics**: Understand how users interact with the app
- **Crash Reporting**: Identify and fix bugs
- **Performance Monitoring**: Optimize app performance
- **Feature Development**: Plan new features based on usage

### Communication
- **Service Updates**: Notify you about app updates or changes
- **Support**: Respond to your questions and requests
- **Important Notices**: Send critical information about the service

---

## How We Share Your Information

### With Other Users
- **Drivers**: Your name and role are visible to other users on the same route
- **Passengers**: Can see driver's name and bus location during active trips
- **Location**: Real-time bus location is shared with passengers on the same route

### With Service Providers
We share information with trusted third-party services:

- **Firebase (Google)**: Authentication, database, cloud functions, analytics
- **WhatsApp Business API (Meta)**: Sending arrival notifications
- **Google Maps**: Displaying maps and location services

### Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law or to:
- Comply with legal obligations
- Protect our rights and safety
- Prevent fraud or security issues
- Respond to government requests

### We DO NOT:
- ❌ Sell your personal information
- ❌ Share your data with advertisers
- ❌ Use your location for marketing
- ❌ Track you when not using the app (except drivers during trips)

## Data Storage and Security

### Where Data is Stored
- **Firebase Firestore**: User profiles, routes, trip data (Google Cloud, US region)
- **Firebase Authentication**: Login credentials (encrypted)
- **Device Storage**: Minimal cached data for offline functionality

### Security Measures
We implement industry-standard security measures:

- **Encryption**: All data transmitted over HTTPS/TLS
- **Authentication**: Secure Firebase Authentication
- **Access Control**: Role-based permissions
- **Regular Updates**: Security patches and updates
- **Monitoring**: Continuous security monitoring

### Data Retention
- **Active Accounts**: Data retained while account is active
- **Location History**: Deleted after trip completion (24 hours)
- **Deleted Accounts**: Data permanently deleted within 30 days
- **Analytics**: Anonymous analytics retained for 14 months

### Access and Control
You have the right to:

- **Access**: View your personal information in the app
- **Update**: Edit your profile information anytime
- **Delete**: Request account deletion
- **Export**: Request a copy of your data

### Location Permissions
You can control location access:

- **Grant/Revoke**: Change permissions in device settings

- **Disable Tracking**: Stop trip to stop location sharing

### Notification Preferences
- **WhatsApp**: Opt-out by not providing phone number
- **Push Notifications**: Disable in device settings
- **Email**: Unsubscribe from emails (except critical updates)
